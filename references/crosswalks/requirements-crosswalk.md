## "Test Once, Comply Many" Opportunities

These areas have HIGH overlap — testing once can satisfy multiple standards:

### 1. Independence (High Overlap)
- **Test**: Collect independence declaration
- **Satisfies**: GAGAS §3.26, IIA 1120, SOX Rule 3520, ISO Principle 5
- **Abstraction**: One independence form with standard-specific questions

### 2. Risk Assessment (High Overlap)
- **Test**: Perform risk assessment for engagement
- **Satisfies**: GAGAS §8.07, IIA 2010, SOX AS 2101, ISO Clause 5
- **Abstraction**: One risk assessment that maps to all standards' requirements

### 3. Evidence Sufficiency (High Overlap)
- **Test**: Gather and document evidence
- **Satisfies**: GAGAS §6.33, IIA 2310, SOX AS 2510, ISO Clause 6.4
- **Abstraction**: One workpaper meets all standards' evidence requirements

### 4. Finding Documentation (Medium Overlap)
- **Test**: Document a finding with all elements
- **Challenge**: GAGAS needs 4 elements, IIA needs 5, SOX needs severity classification
- **Abstraction**: Superset form with 5 elements + severity; render per standard

### 5. Management Response (High Overlap)
- **Test**: Obtain management response
- **Satisfies**: GAGAS §6.55, IIA 2451, SOX remediation plan, ISO CAP
- **Abstraction**: One response workflow serves all standards

### 6. CPE Tracking (Low Overlap)
- **Challenge**: Different hours, cycles, and topic requirements per standard
- **Abstraction**: Unified CPE tracker that validates against multiple standards simultaneously
- **Value**: One auditor's CPE record checked against GAGAS (80/2yr) AND IIA (40/yr) AND CISA (40/yr)
