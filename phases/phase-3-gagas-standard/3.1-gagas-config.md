### 3.1.3 — GAGAS Engagement Types
- [ ] Define engagement types per GAGAS chapters:
  - **Financial Audit** (Chapter 6) — Audit of financial statements
  - **Performance Audit** (Chapter 8) — Effectiveness, efficiency, compliance
  - **Attestation Engagement** (Chapter 7) — Examination, review, agreed-upon procedures
  - **Follow-Up Audit** — Status of prior findings
  - **Special Investigation** — Fraud, waste, abuse
  - **Review** — Limited assurance engagement
