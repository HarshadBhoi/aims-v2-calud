# 3.6 — GAGAS Compliance Crosswalk

**Parent**: [Phase 3 — GAGAS Standard Pack](OVERVIEW.md)
**Estimated effort**: 2 days

---

## Objective

Create crosswalk mappings between GAGAS requirements and other major audit standards. This enables "test once, comply many."

---
