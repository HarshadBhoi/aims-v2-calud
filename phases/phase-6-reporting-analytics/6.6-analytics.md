# 6.6 — Analytics & Trend Analysis

**Parent**: [Phase 6 — Reporting & Analytics](OVERVIEW.md)
**Estimated effort**: 2-3 days

---

## Tasks

- [ ] **Findings Trends**: Findings over time (by month/quarter), by risk rating, by type, by department
- [ ] **Recommendation Resolution**: Average time to resolve, on-time completion rate, by priority
- [ ] **CAP Aging**: Average age, overdue rate, completion trends
- [ ] **Audit Coverage**: Universe entities audited vs total, coverage by risk category
- [ ] **Resource Utilization**: Hours by auditor, by engagement, planned vs actual, utilization %
- [ ] **Engagement Metrics**: Average duration by type, on-time completion, budget variance
- [ ] **QA Metrics**: Common QA findings, QA pass rate, areas needing improvement
- [ ] **Comparative Analysis**: Year-over-year comparison for all metrics
- [ ] **Interactive Charts**: Recharts/Tremor charts with tooltips, drill-down, date range selection
- [ ] **Export Charts**: Download charts as PNG/SVG for presentations
