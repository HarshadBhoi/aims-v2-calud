# 6.1 — Dashboard & KPI Engine

**Parent**: [Phase 6 — Reporting & Analytics](OVERVIEW.md)
**Estimated effort**: 3-4 days

---

## Tasks

- [ ] **Executive Dashboard**: Overview for Directors/Supervisors
  - Active engagements by status (pie/donut chart)
  - Findings by risk rating (bar chart)
  - Open recommendations aging
  - CAP status pipeline
  - Audit plan completion (planned vs actual)
  - Team utilization (hours allocated vs available)
  - Upcoming deadlines
- [ ] **My Dashboard**: Personalized view for each user
  - My engagements (assigned)
  - My pending approvals
  - My time entries this week
  - My CPE progress
  - Recent activity on my items
- [ ] **KPI Cards**: Configurable stat cards
  - Total engagements (YTD)
  - Average engagement duration
  - Finding resolution rate
  - CAP on-time completion %
  - Audit coverage % (universe audited)
  - Budget utilization
- [ ] **Drill-Down**: Click any chart/KPI to see underlying data
- [ ] **Date Range Selector**: Filter all dashboard data by period
- [ ] **Dashboard Customization**: Drag-and-drop widget arrangement (future)
- [ ] **Scheduled Dashboard Email**: Weekly summary to Directors
