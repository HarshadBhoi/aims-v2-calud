# 6.4 — Custom Report Builder

**Parent**: [Phase 6 — Reporting & Analytics](OVERVIEW.md)
**Estimated effort**: 4-5 days

---

## Tasks

- [ ] **Report Builder UI**: Drag-and-drop report designer
  - Select data sources (engagements, findings, recommendations, time, etc.)
  - Choose columns/fields to include
  - Apply filters (date range, status, risk rating, etc.)
  - Group by (engagement, department, auditor, standard, etc.)
  - Sort order
  - Aggregations (count, sum, average)
- [ ] **Report Templates**: Save custom reports as reusable templates
- [ ] **Scheduled Reports**: Run custom reports on a schedule (daily, weekly, monthly)
- [ ] **Output Formats**: Table view, PDF, CSV, Excel
- [ ] **Sharing**: Share custom reports with other users/roles
- [ ] **Chart Integration**: Add charts to custom reports (bar, pie, line, trend)
- [ ] **Saved Filters**: Bookmark commonly used filter combinations
