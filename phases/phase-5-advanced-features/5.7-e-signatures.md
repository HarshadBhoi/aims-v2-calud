# 5.7 — E-Signatures & Digital Sign-Off

**Parent**: [Phase 5 — Advanced Features](OVERVIEW.md)
**Estimated effort**: 2-3 days

---

## Tasks

- [ ] **Sign-Off Mechanism**: Digital signature capturing name, role, timestamp, IP address
- [ ] **Sign-Off Points**: Workpaper review, finding approval, report approval, independence declaration, QA checklist completion
- [ ] **Signature Block**: Rendered in PDFs (name, title, date, "Electronically Signed")
- [ ] **Non-Repudiation**: Signed hash of document content at time of signature
- [ ] **Signature Verification**: Verify that content hasn't changed since signing
- [ ] **Compliance**: Align with ESIGN Act (US), eIDAS (EU) for legal validity
- [ ] **Bulk Sign-Off**: Sign multiple workpapers at once (supervisor bulk review)
- [ ] **Counter-Sign**: Two-party signatures (preparer + reviewer)
- [ ] **Signature Audit Trail**: All signatures logged immutably

---

## Notes

- Start with simple e-signatures (name + timestamp + hash)
- Advanced digital signatures (PKI, certificates) can be added for high-security clients
- Consider DocuSign/Adobe Sign integration for clients who need third-party validation
