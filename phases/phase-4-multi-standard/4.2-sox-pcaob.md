# 4.2 — SOX/PCAOB Standard Pack

> **⚠ Reconciliation 2026-05-01**: DEFERRED from Slice A (covered by Phase 4 OVERVIEW banner). SOX 404 key-control testing is one of the load-bearing use cases for [ADR-0008](../../references/adr/0008-control-matrix-as-separate-model.md) — a SOX key control receives both Test of Design and Test of Effectiveness, which the ControlMatrix-separate-from-AuditTest shape supports without attribute duplication. PCAOB AS 2201 substantive tests still stand alone (no upstream `controlMatrixId` required).

**Parent**: [Phase 4 — Multi-Standard Framework](OVERVIEW.md)
**Estimated effort**: 4-5 days

## Objective

Create the SOX/PCAOB standard pack for public company internal controls over financial reporting (ICFR) audits.

## Key Differences from GAGAS/IIA

| Aspect | GAGAS | IIA | SOX/PCAOB |
|--------|-------|-----|-----------|
| Focus | Government | Internal value | Financial reporting controls |
| Findings | 4 elements | 5 elements | Deficiency classification |
| Risk ratings | Critical-Low | Critical-Advisory | MW/SD/Deficiency |
| Testing | Procedures-based | Risk-based | Control-based (TOD/TOE) |
| Reports | Government report | Management communication | ICFR opinion |
| Regulatory | GAO | IIA | SEC/PCAOB |
